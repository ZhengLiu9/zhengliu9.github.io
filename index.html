I am a Ph.D. student in the Radiological Instrumentation Lab at the Department of Nuclear, Plasma, and Radiological Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My doctoral research has been focused on designing novel sensor network systems for background radiation monitoring and radiation source detection. I am also interested in deep learning and the interdisciplinary research between data science and radiation detection. Please check out the Projects page for more details about my research, and the Blog where I document my hobby projects.
